📊 Executive Summary
In this analysis, I identified key growth drivers and revenue patterns across the retail fleet. My focus was on establishing scalability and tracking KPIs for executive-level reporting.

Total Revenue Analyzed: I processed $698,812 in sales over a 6-month period.
